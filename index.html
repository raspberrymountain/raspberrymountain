Raspberry Mountain Townhouses HOA


Raspberry Mountain Townhouses are conveniently located in Monument, Colorado along I-25 between Colorado Springs and Denver. This ideal location nestled along the Front Range foothills provides residents access to modern conveniences as well as natural Colorado beauty. In addition to the panoramic mountain views, residents will also enjoy Monument Lake, evergreen vistas, and roaming local wildlife in our quiet neighborhood.


Residents: Please log into our management platform TownSquare for documents, updates, payments, and requests!

Want to get involved? Contact the Social Committee at social@raspberrymountain.org!
